Tests Rules and Instructions

Tests Overview

Welcome to the cognitive tests for attention. This progam includes forward and backward digit span tests, as well as a series 7 test.

Digit Span Instructions

  • Wait to enter your answer until all numbers have been displayed
  • After you have submitted your answer wait 5 seconds for the next round to begin
  • All incorrect answers will be counted, the game will continue until all rounds have been finished

Series 7 Instructions

  • Starting at 100 subtract 7 and submit your answer
  • Repeat this until all required calculations have been completed
  • A score will be given based upon the accuracy of the submitted answers
